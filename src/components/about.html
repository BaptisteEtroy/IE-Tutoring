    • About us

    An idea that came about after a casual dinner conversation, I.E. tutoring was launched a year later in March 2024. We are two IE students who decided to build a community around students helping each other. Through our tailored and self-built platform, we enable the connection between students who are seeking help with those providing it. We strive for quality of service for all those involved.

    Our Mission

    At IE Tutoring, we are dedicated to empowering students by providing comprehensive and personalized educational support. We want to unlock your full potential through expert tutoring in so many courses.
